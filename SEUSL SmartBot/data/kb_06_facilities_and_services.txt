CHUNK_ID: KB-FAC-HEALTH-001
TOPIC: Health Centre and Welfare Facilities
CATEGORY: facilities
---
SEUSL Health Centre: Located near the admin block. Staffed by a medical officer who holds regular clinical sessions. Minor ailments treated and medicines provided free of charge. Serious conditions are referred to Kalmunai Base Hospital (~5 km from campus).

University Canteen: Open weekdays serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Food is subsidized for students. Small student-run kiosks and food stalls also operate near hostel areas. Canteen serves halal food (Sri Lankan meals — rice and curry, short eats, beverages). The campus environment is Muslim-friendly with halal food available.

Mosque/Prayer Facilities: Mosque facilities and prayer rooms available on and near campus. Prayer times are respected in the academic schedule.

ATMs and Banking: No ATMs directly on campus. Nearest bank branches and ATMs are in Kalmunai town (~5 km). Bank of Ceylon, People's Bank, and Commercial Bank have branches in Kalmunai.

CHUNK_ID: KB-FAC-FAS-CENTRES-001
TOPIC: FAS Research Centres
CATEGORY: facilities
---
Faculty of Applied Sciences Research Centres:
1. Science Research Centre (SRCFAS): Dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge. Equipped with advanced instrumentation. Research areas: biotechnology, environmental science, materials science, physics, chemistry.
2. Science Education Centre (SECFAS): Links the faculty with the education community. Promotes science education in the southeastern region. Conducts seminars, practical sessions, science camps, quiz competitions, and publishes science monographs.
3. Centre for Soil and Water Research (CSWR): Established in 2004 (first centre at FAS) with external support (NGOs) by Mr. MF Nawas and Mr. EMJM Rizvi. Located at the Department of Chemical Sciences. After the Tsunami, CSWR was actively involved in community services, analyzing affected water wells.
4. Industrial Training Cell (ITC): Established in 2018. Objective: produce science graduates skilled in industrial activities by exposing them to real working environments. Organizes industrial training by linking students with relevant industries.
