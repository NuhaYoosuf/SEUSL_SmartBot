LIBRARY SERVICES:
-----------------
SEUSL has a main university library (Dr. A.M.A. Azeez Library) and a dedicated Science Library.
- Main Library: http://www.seu.ac.lk/library/
- Science Library (FAS): http://www.seu.ac.lk/library/science_library/
The library provides physical and digital resources for students and researchers.
Digital Resources:
- E-books (Subscribed Taylor & Francis eBooks - Access within SEUSL network)
- E-books (Shibboleth/Remote Access for off-campus)
- Library Catalog search (by Title, Author, Subject, ISBN, Series, Call number)
- E-Repository (SEUIR): http://ir.lib.seu.ac.lk/
- E-Databases
Libraries: Main Library, Faculty of Applied Sciences Library
Opening Hours: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

RESEARCH AND INNOVATION CENTRE (RIC):
--------------------------------------
The Research and Innovation Centre (RIC) was established in 2014. The main objective of the RIC serves as the guidepost for the university to reach its full potential in research and scholarly activity which will enhance Sri Lanka's future economic, social and cultural well-being. The RIC promotes and facilitates leading edge, high-quality basic and applied research and development activities including collaborative and interdisciplinary research at University level.
University Statistics: 240+ Academics, 4450+ Students, 6 Faculties, 25+ Departments.
Website: http://www.seu.ac.lk/ric/index.php
