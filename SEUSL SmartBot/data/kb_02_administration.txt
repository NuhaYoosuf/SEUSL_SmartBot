CHUNK_ID: KB-ADM-007
TOPIC: Student Administrative Procedures
CATEGORY: administration
---
Key student administrative procedures at SEUSL:
- Student ID Card: Issued within first few weeks of registration by the Registrar's office. Required for library access, exams, and hostel. Your student index number (e.g., CS/2024/001) is your unique university identifier used on all exam papers, LMS login, library registration, and fee payments.
- Student Confirmation / Status Letter: Submit a written request or fill the form at the Registrar's office stating the purpose (bank account, scholarship, visa, etc.). Issued within 3–5 working days. A small fee may apply. Bring your student ID when collecting.
- Official Academic Transcript: Submit a written request to the Registrar's office with your student index number, full name, degree program, and purpose. Transcripts are usually ready within 5–10 working days. A fee is charged per copy. Official transcripts are sealed and stamped.
- Deferment/Suspension of Studies: Submit a formal written application to the Registrar via your HoD with valid reasons (medical, family emergency) and supporting documents. Medical deferral requires a certified government hospital letter. A maximum of one academic year deferment is usually permitted during the full degree program.
- Grievance: Terms of Reference (ToR) of the Grievance Hearing Committee and Grievance Application Form are available at https://www.seu.ac.lk/guidelines.php
