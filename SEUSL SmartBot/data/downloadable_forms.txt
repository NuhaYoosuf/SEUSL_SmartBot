DOWNLOADABLE FORMS AND DOCUMENTS - SOUTH EASTERN UNIVERSITY OF SRI LANKA
=========================================================================

The following official forms and documents are available for download from the SEUSL website.
Download page: https://www.seu.ac.lk/download.php

HUMAN RESOURCES / STAFF FORMS:
-------------------------------
1. Leave Application Form - For applying sick, vacation, casual, half-day, or no-pay leave.
2. Prior Approval Form (OR/OT/01) - Request for approval to work on Saturday/Sunday/Holiday (overtime).
3. OT Prior Approval - Overtime prior approval request form.
4. Official Email Request Form - Request for an official @seu.ac.lk email address. Students must include their Student Registration Number (e.g., xxxeg23.001@seu.ac.lk).
5. Application Form (Agrahara Medical Scheme) - National Insurance Trust Fund Board medical scheme for semi-government employees.
6. Introducing New Advance Requisition Note - For requesting advance payments.

GUEST HOUSE AND FACILITY BOOKING:
-----------------------------------
7. Form GA/G/01 - Guest House Booking - Application for lodging at university guest houses (Oluvil, Sammanthurai, APC Mt. Lavinia). Specify A/C or Non-A/C rooms.
8. Form GA/G/02 - APC Booking - Booking form for APC (Auditorium/Conference) facility.

TRANSPORT:
----------
9. Form GA/G/04 - Application for Railway Warrant - For official railway travel warrants.

STORES AND PROCUREMENT:
------------------------
10. Form GA/G/05 - Gate Pass for Returnable Items - Gate pass for taking returnable university items off campus.
11. PRN Form for Capital Items - Purchase Requisition Note for capital expenditure items.
12. PRN Form for Consumable Items - Purchase Requisition Note for consumable supplies.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS:
-------------------------
13. ERSU Repair Request - Electronics Repair Service Unit request form for repair/service of CPU, UPS, monitors, printers, laptops, CCTV, photocopiers, and other electronic equipment.
14. Works Order (Form SEU/WMD/01) - Works & Maintenance Department order for electrical, carpentry/masonry, water supply/plumbing, and other maintenance or repair works.

NOTE: For the latest versions of these forms, visit: https://www.seu.ac.lk/download.php
For guidelines and regulations, visit: https://www.seu.ac.lk/guidelines.php
