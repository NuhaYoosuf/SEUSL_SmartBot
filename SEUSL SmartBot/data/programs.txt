UNDERGRADUATE DEGREE PROGRAMS - SOUTH EASTERN UNIVERSITY OF SRI LANKA
======================================================================

FACULTY OF ARTS AND CULTURE (FAC) PROGRAMS:
--------------------------------------------
The Faculty of Arts and Culture offers interdisciplinary programs covering humanities, social sciences, languages, and information technology.

Departments and their study areas:
1. Department of Social Sciences - sociology, anthropology, social research
2. Department of Languages - foreign and local language studies
3. Department of Geography - physical and human geography, geoinformatics
4. Department of Political Science - political systems, governance, public policy
5. Department of Economics and Statistics - economic theory, statistical analysis
6. Department of English Language Teaching (DELT) - English linguistics, teaching methodology
7. Department of Sociology - social structure, culture, community studies
8. Department of Information Technology - computing, ICT applications in arts context

Main Degree: Bachelor of Arts (BA) - 3 to 4-year program

Short Courses / Certificate Programs:
- Diploma in English
- Short Course in Geo-informatics (GIS)
- Certificate in Human Rights

Research Journals:
- KALAM International Research Journal
- Pravaham Journal
- Journal of Social Review (JSR)


FACULTY OF MANAGEMENT AND COMMERCE (FMC) PROGRAMS:
----------------------------------------------------
Following undergraduate pathways are available:

Departments:
1. Department of Accountancy and Finance - financial accounting, auditing, corporate finance
2. Department of Management - human resource management, strategic management, organizational behaviour
3. Department of Management and IT (MIT) - management information systems, e-business, technology management
4. Department of Marketing Management - marketing strategy, consumer behaviour, digital marketing

Main Degrees:
- Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
- Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
- Bachelor of Science in Management and Information Technology

Postgraduate Programs:
- Postgraduate Diploma (PGD)
- MPhil (Master of Philosophy) by Research
- PhD (Doctor of Philosophy) by Research

Entry Requirements for Postgraduate Degrees available for download from: https://www.seu.ac.lk/fmc/downloads/mphil_phd/

FACULTY OF TECHNOLOGY (FT) PROGRAMS:
-------------------------------------
The Faculty consists of two departments:

1. Department of Biosystems Technology (BST):
   - B.Sc. in Biosystems Technology

2. Department of Information and Communication Technology (ICT):
   - B.Sc. in Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
   - Focus: software development, networking, database management, cybersecurity, multimedia

   - Application: https://www.seu.ac.lk/ft/stc.php

GENERAL ADMISSION INFORMATION:
-------------------------------
- Undergraduate admissions are handled by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Sri Lanka
- Admission is based on G.C.E. Advanced Level (A/L) exam results
- Students are selected based on merit and district quotas
- International students can contact the OSPIM office: http://www.seu.ac.lk/ospim/index.php
- UGC website: http://www.ugc.ac.lk/
- Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE): http://www.mohe.gov.lk/
