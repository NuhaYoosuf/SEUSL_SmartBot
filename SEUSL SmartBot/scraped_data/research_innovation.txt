Welcome To RIC

Research and Innovation Centre (RIC)
was established in year 2014. The main objective of the RIC serves as the guidepost for the university to reach its full potential in research and scholarly activity which will enhance Sri Lanka's future economic, social and cultural well-being. Further, the RIC is to promote and facilitate leading edge, high-quality basic and applied research and development activities including collaborative and interdisciplinary research at University level.
