GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS
a. No any articles / goods of the University can be transferred through the main gate without submitting this
Gate Pass to the University security.
b. Any items on its return to South Eastern University of Sri Lanka should be entered in this form which
will be available at the Security Officer, Main Gate and duly signed by the officer who returned the item
to the University.
c. Three copies of the Gate Pass should be prepared. One copy should be retained by the Originating
Department, one copy has to be sent to the Administration Division, and the other copy has to be
handed over to the Security Gate.
